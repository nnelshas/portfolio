## Summary
- Bayesian uncertainty quantification and deployment-focused inference optimization for a vision-language action model (OpenVLA).

## Highlights
- Implemented Bayesian LoRA and Laplace approximation for out-of-distribution uncertainty estimation.
- Optimized inference to **99.2% success** on **LIBERO** via confidence-based dynamic allocation of open-loop actions.
